Meet The Team

Lawrence Redfern


Lawrence Redfern (s3748752) Is a ¼ Aboriginal and ¾ Caucasian who is apart of the TGIPD team. He has always had a passion in technology and has experimented with code to create small games. Gaming was his introduction into technology, he wondered at a young age how games were made but never thought to research it. His hobbies consist of Photography, Motorcycles, Gaming and Coding. Liam Chan-Wicht


Liam Chan-Wicht (s3803217) is a half Chinese, half Australian member of TGIPD, born in Sydney. He designs logos and other graphic stuff in his spare time for friends and clients. Liam has always been interested in IT, from sticking pencils into expensive laptops as a kid to learning to code games and websites as an adult. Video Gaming has always been a large part of his life and it wouldn’t be possible without the ever growing field of IT. He attended a coding bootcamp a couple years ago that covered HTML/CSS, Ruby/Ruby on Rails, Javascript including MEAN/MERN stacks and decided that he needed a fuller education to pursue a career in the IT field. He's very excited for the next 30-40 years as he believes innovations such as Artificial Intelligence and Quantum Computing will drastically change our way of living.

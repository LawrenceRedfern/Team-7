Group Processes

David Pipczak

The group worked quite well together on assignment 2, the group come together early and set out achievable realistic goals, they used communication tools such as discord, Trello and GitHub to bring it all together, and even though there was one absent group member, the team kept on pushing through and working hard, the team did not let this stop them and since that time the absent team member had removed himself from the course and our team.

Johnathon Tassoni

The group worked together very well for Assignment 2. All group members were welcoming and inclusive and all members were able to complete their individual tasks on time. The group quickly organised a method of communication and a way to share completed task work. Unfortunately there was a case of an absent team member, but the team was able to overcome this hurdle and pull together. At the conclusion of Assignment 2, the group decided that it would be best to have more frequent communication going forward for assignment 3.

Lawrence Redfern

The group worked hand in hand for assignment 2 and dealt with the inactive member in the best way possible. We all completed our duties well before the deadline and used the remaining time to read over everything and make sure it was what we envisioned. I feel as though a more frequent meeting would be best for the group and allowing the group to make changes to others work where the whole group sees fit.

Johnathon Tassoni

For our project we will need:

Project manager : The role of the project manager will be to lead and co-ordinate the group. They will be responsible for ensuring that all members are keeping on schedule with their allocated task and managing the team members. They should have a clear picture of where the project is at and what needs to be done next.

Hardware engineer : As we are utilising a Raspberry Pi - we will need someone who is proficient with the technology. They will have to have a firm understanding of the components of the Pi and how to solve and hardware issues associated with it.

Software developer/Programmer : we require a member who specialises in programming to be able to program the functionality required. It would be preferable for the team member to have a large amount of programming experience under their belt and to have knowledge of integrating the hardware of the Raspberry Pi with their program.

Graphic Designer : As we must pitch/sell our project we will need someone to handle the design of the teams logos, website graphics and any further graphics required for official team documents. They should have knowledge and be proficient with multiple graphic software (Photoshop, Illustrator, etc).

Liam Chan-Wicht

4 Person Team (with fear of sounding redundant):

Hardware Engineer: The Raspberry Pi is a particular piece of hardware that we have chosen to use to fix this problem. Someone with knowledge of the ins and outs of the hardware will be needed to successfully deliver.

Software Engineer: Whilst the hardware engineer will have some capability with the interaction of the Raspberry Pi, a software engineer will have a greater knowledge of interactions between programs and lines of code. This person will have to be as knowledgeable as the hardware engineer to effectively interact with the Pi and other software/hardware.

Project Lead: This role must be filled by someone who can organise themselves as well as the other team members. In production, deadlines must be met and the project lead is the person to encourage the members to push for these deadlines. They must also have a clear understanding of how the project is progressing and how each interaction will affect the outcome of the product.

General Purpose Designer: In front of all the code and hardware, there must be a bright shiny box/website/label/EDM/piece of advertising that alerts other potential investors to the product. Knowledge of the Adobe suite will be beneficial, as well as knowledge of wire-framing, prototyping and other modeling processes.
